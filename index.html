WHAT WE DO

The role of the Design Collective is to select cohorts of aspiring designers and pair them up with seasoned product designers in the tech industry. Each mentor-mentee pairing will work on a real-world design problem of the mentor’s choosing during a 4/6-week period where students will finish their project, write a case study, and receive feedback/mentorship throughout.

These design problems will be different from random, hypothetical problems interviewers might use during design interviews—these are problems mentors may see in their industries and are impacting real people.
